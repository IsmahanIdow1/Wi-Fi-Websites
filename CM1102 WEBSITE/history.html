Who Created Wi-Fi?

Wi-Fi was invented by Dr.John O'Sulivan as he worked in a team to then came into real life in 1991. This was then patented in Australia
in 1992 and then in the US in 1996 which then lead to high speed wireless connection that we have now. Then we seen in 1999 WECA took over
to make this more accessible to users to use all over the world.

We see in the begining Wi-Fi was an accidental creation. Researchers were trying to figure out a solution in radio astronomy.
While they were doing this they made a chip that cleaned up radio signals. Which then aided them in sending data without wire,
which then lead to Wi-Fi being made.

The problems that comes with Wi-Fi

We see that Wi-Fi has had great progression throughtout the years but it hasn't been as easy as it seen.
The main problem their has been is the risk of hacking that has come with Wi-Fi networks.
So although their is great progress cyber attack is a main issue which then make it hard for wireless connection to be 100% safe.

Futhermore, Wi-Fi also faces problems with the speed and connectivity this is because we see Wi-Fi use direct line of sight
and it can easily be intervne with when it comes signal. Which then lower the range and makes connecting slower.
We see this as if alot of people are trying to connect to one network it seems to lag and the speed slows down which make it impractical to use.
