Details of Wi-Fi

Wi-Fi Standards and Frequency

Wi-Fi uses IEEE 802.11 protocool and works with Ethernet.
The devices connect using WAP (Wireless Access Point).
Most Wi-Fi uses 2.4 GHz and the later versions uses 6 GHz whiles the older ones used 5 GHz.
So then one equitment can carry channels at a time.

Wi-Fi Range and Line-of-Sight Weakenesses

Wi-Fi works using line of sight,so if there is something disrupting the line of sight then the signal will be reduced.
A normal access point can range to around 20 meters when it is inside but some specific can go up to 150 when outside.
Whereas a hotspot can only cover one room. But we seen as the years went on that Wi-Fi speed has upgraded
and in 2019 some even reach up to "9.6 Gbits/s" at close proximity.
